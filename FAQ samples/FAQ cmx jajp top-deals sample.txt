Question
Can l apply a membership discount towards a deals purchase?
SEO Description
Exceptions for some products may apply, but member discounts like students discounts, military discounts and others can be applied to deals purchases. Before purchasing, check the product details for specific exceptions that may apply. Visit Membership Programs for more information about discount offers.
Question
Can l earn and redeem Dell Rewards points with a deals purchase?
SEO Description
Yes, you can earn and redeem Dell Rewards points on all products in deals. Visit Dell Rewards to learn how to earn and redeem rewards.
Question
Are support services and warranties offered for products in deals?
SEO Description
Yes, support services and warranties are available for products in deals. However, deals and special offers for support services and warranties may differ by product. Visit Support Services and Warranty for more information.
Question
Do products in deals include free delivery?
SEO Description
Yes, all products in deals get free standard shipping with no required minimum purchase. Additional expedited shipping options may be available for selected products and delivery locations. Visit Dell Shipping and Delivery FAQs for more information.
Question
What is the difference between Dell Outlet and deals?
SEO Description
Dell Outlet sells certified refurbished and overstocked Dell products at a discount, whereas deals provide discounts and special offers on new, never-owned Dell products. Visit Dell Outlet to browse discounted refurbished and overstocked products.
